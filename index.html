"The word ‘female,’ when inserted in front of something, is always with a note of surprise. Female COO, female pilot, female surgeon — as if the gender implies surprise … One day there won’t be female leaders. There will just be leaders."

Sheryl Sandberg — Facebook COO and Founder of Leanin.org

The world is constantly changing, and its people change with it. Our conception of life, attitude towards differences and diversities has all formed during the years. There are some things that still sound surprising to a lot of people, like pineapple on pizza or women in tech. Nowadays women have more and more opportunities to widen their knowledge of coding and programming, and has the chance to gain experience and job-roles in technological fields, but we still have shortcomings.

This is why we encourage YOU to step up and if you feel like coding is your path in life don't hesitate to make steps towards it. We are here to give you some guidance, and help.

If you have any question browse through our FAQs, or feel free to ask your own question in email by contacting us via womencoding@gmail.com
